About Us

Serendevity is a custom software development company made up of highly-qualified, talented and innovative IT professionals each with their own area of expertise. Their combined experience covers full range of custom software development - from start ups to complex systems.

The name Serendevity (ser·en·dev·i·ty | ser-ən-ˈdi-və-tē) is a wordplay on the more transcendant word Serendepity : luck that takes the form of finding valuable or pleasant things that are not looked for.

Serendevity works like an extension of the client. It augments the company with a team of talent, experience, and organization. The client benefits from a team to support its applications – a redundancy that is needed to minimize risk in running crucial applications. An individual can only excel in so many things whereas a team is made up of professional with particular strong suits, whether they are front end, back-end, database, design, or mobile.
